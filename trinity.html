Trinity University

Trinity University is a division three school in San Antonio, Texas with outstanding facilities and a beautiful campus. The university offers a big range of classes with quality professors and a high standard of education.


Photo courtesy of Trinity University

The features about Trinity that I appreciate most are the high quality soccer programme and the help and guidance which is offered. I feel like there is always someone to go to if I am needing help with a subject and the people are very friendly. The bell center facilities are also top quality along with an outstanding soccer field to play on.
